Welcome to Cordelia.

We are the adventure lovers home base in the magnificent Nopolo, Loreto Bay area in the Baja. Not an ordinary home base, but an extraordinary hotel where all the little luxuries you crave have been curated into one boutique experience. Artisan designed and built furnishings, luxury bedding and spa treatments, mouth watering regional and internationally inspired cuisine crafted from local, sustainable ingredients all accompanied by specially selected wines and signature crafted cocktails. Rooftop dining and pool overlooking the bay and epic sunsets help you refresh for tomorrow’s adventure. Aventuras, our sister company, will book you in for your chance to experience the Baja terrain you have heard so much about. Specialized E-bike, KTM dirt bike, and Speed UTV’s with expert guides are at your fingertips. And if you need ‘a day off’ just ask about the fishing, paddle boarding, snorkelling, hiking, golf and sightseeing.
